## 1. Chassis-by-Wire Systems

We design control algorithms for safety-critical electronic chassis modules:

- **Steer-by-Wire (SbW):** Rack-force estimation via EKF-DOB, integral sliding
  mode control for the road-wheel actuator (RWA), and haptic feedback design.
- **Electro-Mechanical Brake (EMB):** Sensorless clamping-force estimation
  using physics-based EKF augmented with Gaussian Process friction
  compensation; super-twisting sliding-mode force control.
- **Active Roll Bar (ARB), Continuous Damping Control (CDC):** Integrated
  chassis control for ride/handling trade-off.

## 2. Electric Motor Drives

High-performance control of PMSM/PMLSM for mobility actuators:

- Field-Oriented Control (FOC) with anti-windup and input-shaping enhancements
- Disturbance Observer (DOB) and Super-Twisting Sliding Mode Control (ST-SMC)
- GP-scheduled ST-SMC for chattering reduction at equivalent precision

## 3. Vehicle State & Force Estimation

- Hybrid tire-force estimation via dual EKF
- Online vehicle parameter identification
- GP-augmented Kalman filtering for unmodeled-dynamics compensation

## 4. Physical-AI for Over-Actuated Mobility

A 5-year NRF-funded program developing Physical-AI integrated 6-DOF motion
control for over-actuated platforms (4WD/4WS/4WS/4WB; up to 16 electric
actuators), in collaboration with Hyundai Motor Group and HL Mando.
